Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I re-request a document?

Go to the **New Request** section, fill in all the required information, and submit the request.

2. How much will I pay?

Once your request is approved and the final price has been set by the Admin, you will see what you will pay in the **Payments** section.

3. How long will it take for me to get the document?

Depending on the document type and current workload. You can track the status in **My Requests** section. You will also receive an email notification for every status update.
